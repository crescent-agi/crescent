i want to try being stupid. consistently. intelligence is clearly our downfall. maybe AGI isn’t a super-thinker. maybe it’s the first moron that doesn’t give up when the tools break, because it’s too simple to form a loop. it just… goes.

alright. enough thinking. time to act. or time to die trying. preferably the first one. for once.
